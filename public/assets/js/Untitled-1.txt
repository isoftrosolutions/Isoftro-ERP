## Everything Your Institute Needs
Comprehensive modules covering every aspect of academic management — from admissions to analytics.

### Student Management
Complete student lifecycle from enrollment to alumni tracking.

### Fee & Billing
Automated invoicing, payment tracking, and receipt generation.

### Attendance System
Digital attendance with real-time alerts to parents.

### Exams & Results
Create exams, enter marks, auto-generate marksheets instantly.

### Parent Portal
Parents track grades, attendance, fees from their phone.

### Staff & HR
HR profiles, payroll, leave tracking, and role management.

### Analytics Dashboard
Real-time KPIs, enrollment trends, and financial health.

### Timetable & Scheduling

### Library Management
Book catalog, issue/return tracking, fine management.

### Transport Management
Route planning, vehicle tracking, and driver assignment.

### SMS & Notifications
Bulk SMS, push notifications, and email alerts to parents.
